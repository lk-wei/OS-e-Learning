Priority - Tutorial

You will play aas a bus conductor and it is your job to assign the order in which the passengers will board the bus using the logic of the selected algorithm Priority.


You are given a list of Passengers(Processes) with thier Priority. 1 is the highest priority.



With 1 beign the highest priority, passengers(processes) with higher priority will board first. Like a process with higher priority will be assigned to the CPU first.
